About Me

Hi, my name is Budge. I am a young budgerigar born at the end of 2015. I was adopted by Mat and Steph a few days after New Year's because I was so dang adorable. If you haven't noticed, this site has been themed after me because I always crave attention even when Mat and Steph just want to eat in peace.

Things I like to do? Gnaw on everything! My bell? Gnaw on it. Your finger? Gnaw on it. Your ears? Gnaw on it. You get the picture. In my free time I like to be a spider-bird and climb all over my cage whether it's sideways, upside down, or any new contortion I can discover. I also enjoy ringing my bell at all times of day.

Millet is my favorite treat and I can be bribed to do anything for it. If you bring me out to play, you better have some of that millet ready. Mmmmm.
